Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15 August 2025

1. Introduction

Jai Ganesh (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

2. Camera Permission

Our app may request the android.permission.CAMERA permission only for store owner/admin use in the admin section of the app to manage product catalogue images.

  • Regular users/customers are never asked for camera access.
  • The app does not capture, store, or access any camera or gallery images from customers.

3. Information We Collect

  • From Customers: No camera images, gallery images, or personal data are collected.
  • From Admins: The admin may take product photos for catalogue purposes. These are managed solely within the store’s system.

4. Data Usage

Any product images uploaded by the admin are used only for displaying items in the store’s catalogue. We do not share, sell, or rent images or personal data to third parties.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect all stored product images from unauthorized access.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted here with the revised date.
