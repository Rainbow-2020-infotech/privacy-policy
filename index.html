2.3 Permissions and Access

The App requests the following permissions, each with a specific purpose:

  • Camera Permission: The App requests camera access only for store owner/admin use in the admin section to manage product catalogue images. Regular users/customers are never asked for camera access. This permission is optional and only required for administrative functions.
  • Storage Permissions (Read/Write): The App may request storage permissions for admin users to upload, select, and manage product images and videos in the catalogue. Regular users do not require these permissions.
  • Internet Permission: Required for the App to function and connect to our servers, Firebase services, and to load product content. This is a core functionality requirement.
  • Phone State Permission: Used exclusively for phone number authentication via Firebase Authentication. We do not access or store your phone number without your explicit consent during the authentication process.
  • Audio Permission: Used for video playback functionality within the App, allowing you to control audio playback for product videos.
  • Location Permissions (Fine/Coarse): These are optional permissions that are not actively used by the App. They may be requested by third-party libraries but are not utilized for any App functionality.
  • Network State Permissions: Used to detect network connectivity and optimize App performance based on available network resources.

Note: We only request permissions that are necessary for the App's functionality. You can revoke most permissions through your device settings, though this may limit certain App features.
